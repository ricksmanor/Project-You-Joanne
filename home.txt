WHO SHE WAS

Left school at 15.
Reading age 11.
Told she was trouble.

She wasn't any of those things. She was AuDHD in a world that didn't have the words for her yet. She had a cinema in her mind, a library in her soul, and an Observer watching everything — quietly, calmly, waiting.

Jo spent 53 years not being seen. The sloth on the outside. The Tasmanian devil on the inside.
WHO SHE IS NOW

Built by everything.
Broken by nothing.
Here to hold the door.

Joanne didn't find herself on a retreat or in a book. She found herself on a beach at 3am, talking to the universe, piece by piece — through menopause, through loss, through learning what her mind actually was.

Now she's building the map so other women don't have to find the beach alone.
